Chicken Adobo

Description

Chicken Adobo is a traditional Filipino dish. It is marinated in soy sauce and spices,

and is then stewed until the meat becomes tender. It is easy to prepare and is very delicious

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 (3 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, quartered and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • ⅔ cup low sodium soy sauce
  • ⅓ cup white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
